About Our Electronics

"Quality without destroying the enviroment"


Here in Pear Electronics, we stick by our motto of preserving our already fragile enviroment. Many companies have no regard for the enviroment, and carelessly pollutes the atmosphere. We use a variety of different methods to reduce our impact on the enviroment.

Manufacturing

The tools that we use to manufacture our products are all natural and does not pollute the enviroment. They consist of pocket knives, glue, and stock images from the web.

We also use enviromentally friendly techniques like:

  • Bringig the enviromental cost of manufacturing our electronics to 0 by using no phones for customer service and forcing you to pick up your own electronics.
  • Killing no insects.
  • Killing no animals.
  • Killing no humans. (Mostly anyways)
  • Using enviromentally friendly glue to put different materials together. Simply put, we just give you a pile of scraps.
